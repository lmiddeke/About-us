Find your path.

Planning for your future can be scary.
The Genius Compass is an organization that aims to help high school students learn more about their future careers. It is our goal to help students determine which careers interest them in order to plan their academic career.

Build the Connection.

Seeing the worth in your studies can be difficult. At The Genius Compass, we place an emphasis on building connections between high school education and real world applications through career-related learning.

Mission & Vision Statement

Mission Statement: We strive to help students discover new career pathways and improve accessibility to education.

Vision Statement: To allow for all learners to find what they are passionate about and enjoy learning.
